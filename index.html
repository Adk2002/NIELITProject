Help and Instruction

Welcome to the Assam State Higher Education Student Portal!
Please follow these instructions to access the student portal, where you can find essential resources, digital ID card, programme details and more:
In case you are a first-time user of the portal:
Click on ‘New Registration’ button above to generate your log in credentials.
If you have registered earlier:
You can log in directly by entering your enrolment/roll number credentials for the portal, your password and the captcha in the login form.
In case you have forgotten your password:
Please use the link below to reset your password.
Note:
If you are having any difficulties in the registration/login, please contact your college administrator and verify that the entered details are correct.
